# LegalEase Pro — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2024  
**App:** LegalEase Pro (com.legalease.pro)  
**Operator:** LegalEase Pro Team, India

This Privacy Policy describes how LegalEase Pro ("we", "us", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide
- **Phone number** — only for OTP-based authentication. We do NOT use it for marketing.
- **Name and state** (optional) — for personalization and jurisdiction-specific advice.
- **Email** (optional) — for account recovery.
- **Chat messages and queries** — stored to enable conversation history.
- **Complaint draft inputs** — stored so you can review/edit drafts later.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Device ID** — anonymized identifier to associate sessions with your device.
- **App usage logs** — minimal logs (which screen, when) for performance and crash analysis.
- **API request logs** — IP address, timestamp, endpoint (retained 30 days, then deleted).

### 1.3 Information we DO NOT collect
- Location, contacts, photos, microphone (except voice-to-text within your control), call logs, SMS.
- Sensitive personal data (Aadhaar, PAN, financial details).
- Court documents or any uploaded documents are processed in-memory and not stored.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate you via OTP.
- To remember your chat conversations.
- To improve our laws database and AI responses (in aggregated, anonymized form only).
- To send service notifications (e.g., "session expired").

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties.
- Use your data for behavioural advertising.
- Share with law enforcement except under court order.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| Google Gemini AI | AI responses | Your legal question text (no PII) |
| Indian Kanoon | Case law search | Search query only |
| LiveLaw, Bar & Bench, SCObserver | News feed | None (we fetch public RSS only) |
| eCourts services (deeplinks) | Case tracking | None — links open in browser |

OTP delivery (in production) will use Twilio/MSG91/Fast2SMS — your phone number is shared with them for that purpose only.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- Data is stored on encrypted servers within India.
- Passwords are not stored — we use OTP-only authentication.
- JWT tokens are signed with HS256 and expire in 30 days.
- All API traffic is over HTTPS.
- Database backups are encrypted at rest.

## 5. Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data — request a copy via support@legalease.pro
- **Correct** your information in the app or via support.
- **Delete** your account and all associated data — tap "Clear all local data" in Settings, or email support.
- **Withdraw consent** — uninstall the app.
- **Lodge a complaint** with the Data Protection Board (post DPDPA 2023 implementation).

## 6. Data Retention

- Chat history: kept until you delete it.
- Account data: kept until you delete account.
- API logs: 30 days, then auto-deleted.
- OTPs: 5 minutes (then auto-expired).

## 7. Children

LegalEase Pro is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Adults may seek legal information about minors (POCSO, juvenile justice, etc.).

## 8. Disclaimer of Legal Advice

**IMPORTANT:** AI responses are **informational only** and do NOT constitute legal advice. The App is not a substitute for a licensed advocate. Always consult a qualified lawyer for serious matters. The App and its operators are NOT liable for any decisions taken based on the information provided.

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
